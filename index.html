Who We Are

We are a leading manufacturer, supplier, and exporter of zippers, sliders, and garment accessories. Our products are approved by ITS and SGS testing labs.

We offer high-quality zippers at competitive prices, with reliable delivery within the promised timeframe. Our fully equipped manufacturing plant can meet demand across a wide range of zipper types, including invisible, nylon, coil, plastic moulded, and metal zippers. All zippers are produced and dyed in-house using high-temperature, high-pressure dyeing systems, ensuring consistent performance and compliance with international quality standards.

Business Philosophy

Driven by the ambition to meet the ever-changing requirements of the market, we are committed to offering excellent products with an easy ordering process.
